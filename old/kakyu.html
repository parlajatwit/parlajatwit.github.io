Kakyuumari

back

Kakyuumari was my very first game! I wanted to enter the Ludum Dare game jam because my friends were participating.
I sought out an artist and a musician from the event's Discord server, then got to work.
We wanted to make a game that was similar to Katamari Damacy, a game where you push around a sticky ball, picking up objects.
You can play it here. Be warned, it's a bit buggy.
