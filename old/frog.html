American Ballfrog

back

American Ballfrog was my submission to the Ballfrog animation contest on Newgrounds. It won first place, with a cash prize.
On this project, I wrote the script, created and animated the characters, did some prop design for the set, and edited audio.


The idea to make this short be an American Psycho parody stemmed from wanting to make something different. I figured most of the other entries would be tame and play it safe, trying to create something akin to a trailer for the ballfrog game. So I challenged the status quo and made a parody that puts this swampland inhabitant in the scariest place of all: an office job.
While not quite a direct parody, it heavily references the business card scene from American Psycho. I quite enjoyed the dynamic between Patrick Bateman and Paul Allen, so the characters are loosely inspired by them.
If you haven't seen American Psycho, this could best be described as a tired office worker dealing with a nagging coworker.
